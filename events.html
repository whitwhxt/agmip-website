CalLMIP 1st Phase Launch Community Webinar

The 1st Phase of CalLMIP will officially be launched on 24th November 2025. An hour long virtual webinars will be held in two time zones. During the webinar we will present updates on the CalLMIP 1st phase planning, the final protocol, plan for papers, and information for modeling groups who wish to participate.

We will post a link to a recording of the event here after the workshop.
